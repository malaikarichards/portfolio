USF Application Splash Page

I recreated a landing page that helps USF applicants easily identify available majors and the USF campuses that offer them. I made a HubSpot template that leverages HubDB to accommodate the extensive range of programs. I also added a dynamic search bar that filters options as they type. The HubDB configuration allows quick updates by any team member, meeting the campus partners' demands throughout the year. We also made sure to include a dazzling display with microinteractions and animations, as this is the first introduction many users have with USF.

Highlights & Challenges

  • Microinteractions and animations throughout the experience
  • Dynamic major search while user types
  • Conversion of campuses into a collapsible menu on mobile
  • Hubspot Database implementation for ease of data maintenance
